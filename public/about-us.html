About Datagrid

Datagrid is an AI-first software engineering company. We don't just use AI as a buzzword — it's woven into how we design, build, test, and operate every product we ship. From AI-assisted development to production GenAI, intelligent automation is part of our DNA.

We build scalable, maintainable products that grow with our clients — from first MVP to enterprise platform. Our cross-functional pods of senior engineers, designers, and AI specialists bring deep domain expertise across 10 industries and 15+ countries.

Trusted by 750+ global businesses, we partner for the long term — combining technical excellence with genuine care for our clients' success. ISO 9001 & ISO 27001 certified, we deliver with the quality, security, and accountability that lasting partnerships demand.
